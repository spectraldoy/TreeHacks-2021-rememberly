Suspicious in nature, he walked in cautiously and saw a big black bear. The bear was tugging as the cork of a molasses jar with his teeth then it opened and molasses spilled all over the wooded floor. The bear smeared it with his paws and the man screamed, startling the bear. It jumped up on it's two back feet and ran out the door to the lake shore waving his sweet sticky paws in the air.
